Judy Darnell '06 MPP

Assemblymember John Laird has named Judy Darnell as Woman of the Year for the 27th Assembly District. As state advocacy director of the United Way Covering California's Children program, Judy has collaborated with 18 United Way organizations and other partners throughout the state to inform decision makers in Sacramento of the pressing need to provide health insurance for all children in California.

Judy accepted the award at a ceremony on March 5, 2007 at the California State Capitol. The success of Judy's advocacy work has helped make the expansion of children's coverage a top priority of health care reform proposals with both the governor and the state Legislature this year.
